I studied English and some computer science during my undergraduate days, but upon graduating, I decided to make my living as a copy editor and proofreader. While these two disciplines are of course very different (typos can be more devastating in code, for example), I like to think that I will retain some amount of my ability to catch mistakes in my new field.

In the past year, I began working as a nursing assistant, having considered a career in healthcare. While I have decided against pursuing that idea any further, I've been glad to have the chance to work on my feet again, as strange as that may sound.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, sketching with pen, and running.
